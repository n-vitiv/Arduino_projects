Individual work for subject DPKPP

Topic: Design a microcontroller-based device that performs at least 3 functions

Functions of device:
 - measuring temperature and humidity
 - Display of measured information on the LCD display.
 - At high humidity values, turn on the buzzer

Libs to install:
 - DHT.h
 - LiquidCrystal_I2C.h

Needed components for this device:
 - Arduino UNO
 - buzzer
 - DHT11
 - LCD display with I2C interface
 - resistor
